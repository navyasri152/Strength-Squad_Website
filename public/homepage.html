F.A.Q

  • What is yoga?

    Yoga is a holistic practice that combines physical postures, breathing exercises, meditation, and ethical principles to promote overall health and well-being.

  • What is the purpose of yoga asanas?

    Yoga asanas are designed to improve flexibility, strength, balance, and promote physical health.

  • What is the goal of meditation in yoga?

    The goal of meditation in yoga is to quiet the mind, reduce stress, and gain mental clarity and inner peace.

  • How can yoga benefit one's health?

    Yoga can improve physical fitness, reduce stress, enhance flexibility, balance the nervous system, and promote mental and emotional well-being.

  • What is the significance of the lotus position in yoga?

    The lotus position, or "Padmasana," is a seated posture that is associated with meditation and symbolizes purity and spiritual awakening.
